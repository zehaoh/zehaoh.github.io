🍬 Candy Bean Party 🍭

A Sweet and Silly Multiplayer Mining Adventure

Game Overview

Welcome to Candy Bean Party — a delightful multiplayer game where colorful bean characters race, dig, and mine their way to sweet success! Compete with friends, dodge candy traps, and collect shiny gems in a wacky candyland world!

How to Play

  • Choose your favorite bean character
  • Join a mining party with players from around the world
  • Dig deep to find gems while avoiding traps and rivals
  • Use power-ups to sabotage others or boost yourself
  • Win by collecting the most gems before time runs out!

Features

🎮 Fast-paced 4-player matches
🎨 Customize your bean with outfits and accessories
🧠 Strategic digging and skill-based traps
🌍 Global matchmaking and party mode with friends
